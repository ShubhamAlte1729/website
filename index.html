About & Education

I am Shubham Babasaheb Alte, a Ph.D. research scholar in the Department of Mathematics at IIT Indore. My research, under the guidance of Dr. Mohd. Arshad, is focused on Information Theory, with a specific emphasis on information measures, entropy-based analysis, and blind image quality assessment.

My academic journey at IIT Indore began with an M.Sc. in Mathematics, which I later converted into my current Ph.D. program. My work involves both theoretical exploration of concepts like Rényi and Tsallis entropy and practical application in fields like cryptography.
